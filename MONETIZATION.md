## ⚠️ Legal & Compliance

### Required Disclosures (Canada)
- **Affiliate Disclosure:** "We may receive compensation when you click on links to products from our partners"
- **Advertising Standards:** Must disclose paid partnerships (CSA guidelines)
- **Privacy:** PIPEDA compliance for email collection
- **Terms of Use:** Required disclaimers about financial advice

### Best Practices
- Don't claim "best rate" without substantiation
- Include "see issuer website for terms" disclaimers
- Clearly mark sponsored content
- Keep affiliate link tracking transparent
